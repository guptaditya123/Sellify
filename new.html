Our Story

Tradition in Every Pot

Established in 2018, Champaran Matka Mutton brings the authentic taste of Bihar's famous clay pot mutton to the heart of Mumbai. Our journey began with a passion to preserve the traditional cooking methods passed down through generations.

We source the freshest ingredients and locally-raised mutton, marinated with a secret blend of spices for over 24 hours. Each matka (clay pot) is handcrafted by local artisans and sealed with wheat dough before being slow-cooked in wood-fired ovens.

The result? Mutton so tender it falls off the bone, infused with the earthy aroma of clay and the complex flavors of traditional Champaran spices.
